Bourbon Balls
2 tablespoons cocoa
2 cups crushed vanilla wafers
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup finely chopped pecans
¼ cup bourbon, rum or whisky
2 tablespoons of light corn syrup
½ cup melted butter

• Separately crush vanilla wafers and nuts in a Cuisinart then mix them together. Set aside.
• Sift cocoa and powdered sugar into a bowl.
• In a separate bowl, combine the butter, corn syrup, and bourbon.
• Add this liquid mixture to the cocoa and powdered sugar and whisk together.
• Now add the crushed nuts and vanilla wafers and combine well.
• Roll into small balls – about ¾ “diameter.
• Drop into a bowl of powdered sugar and roll around to coat all sides
• Allow to dry for several hours before using.
• Freezes well.
Yield: 45-50
